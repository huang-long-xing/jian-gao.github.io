Research

My current research interests are at the intersection of optimization algorithms, statistics and big data. Most the applications are in large scale transportation systems, with the goal of making the research as impactful as possible.

Publications
  • Bertsimas, D., Delarue, A., Jaillet, P., & Martin, S. Travel Time Estimation in the Age of Big Data. Operations Research (major revision)
  • Bertsimas, D., Jaillet, P., & Martin, S. Online vehicle routing: the edge of optimization in large-scale applications. Operations Research (minor revision)
  • Reilly, J., Martin, S., Payer, M., & Bayen, A. M. (2016). Creating complex congestion patterns via multi-objective optimal freeway traffic control with application to cyber-security. Transportation Research Part B: Methodological, 91, 366-382.
