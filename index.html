I am an Assistant Professor in Operations at the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University.

My research is at the interface of analytics, transportation and public policy. Specifically, I design scalable algorithms and decision making tools that both improve transportation efficiency and positively affect society.

Notably, I work with school districts (San Francisco, Boston) to improve their transportation policies and save millions of dollars, using algorithm to design transparent and equitable policies. I am also a consultant for the ridesharing company Lyft, for which I develop and implement algorithms and products that positively impact millions of drivers and passengers.
